Code Of Conduct

Ukulele Tuesday is a diverse community of all genders, nationalities, ages, and orientations. Music brings us together. We have have a no tolerance policy against racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or any other form of discrimination.

By joining any of our events or online spaces, you agree to abide by the code of conduct below.

Governance

Ukulele Tuesday is a volunteer-run unincorporated association under Irish law, governed by the terms of its club constitution. We are a community group rather than a company or registered charity, and the association itself does not have a separate legal personality. Contracts, financial arrangements and assets are handled by named, elected members of the club's Executive Body (the "trustees"), acting on behalf of the group.

This information is provided to explain how Ukulele Tuesday is organised and is not legal advice.
