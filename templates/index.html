Play Along With Us

Bring your ukulele (or borrow one of ours) and join our big, friendly singalong where everyone plays together. Play, sing, or just soak up the chaos. Come upstairs to The Stag's Head (Dame Lane) every Tuesday from 8pm. Free entry, all levels welcome.

See Us Live

When we're not jamming, we take the ukuleles on the road — bringing our big singalong energy to concerts, festivals, and special events across Ireland (and beyond!). Catch us at the Temple Bar TradFest, the Ukulele Hooley, Galway Uke Fest, and even Monopolele.
