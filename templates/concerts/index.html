Ukulele Tuesday (known to our friends as UT) started life as a small jam in the Stag’s Head pub, Dublin, and has grown into one of the driving forces behind Ireland’s ukulele scene

Ukulele Tuesday is a regular feature at music festivals across the island of Ireland (and beyond!), including appearances at the Temple Bar Trad Fest (IRE), the Ukulele Hooley (IRE), Monopolele Festival (ITA) and TedxFulbrightDublin.

Ukulele Tuesday harnessed the power of social media to score a number of viral hits with songs like “Psycho Killer” by the Talking Heads, “Hit Me Baby” by Britney Spears, and a viral Christmas hit in 2018 with a UT original tune. You can check out our music on Spotify and YouTube.
